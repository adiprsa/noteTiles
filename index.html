NoteTiles

Simple. Free. Online.

No payment, no downloads, no fuss. Just a simple note taking app available on any device, wherever you are.

Sharing is Caring.

Send and receive notes with ease or invite someone to share your notes. Whether it’s work colleagues or friends they can easily see all of your thoughts. Don’t want your notes edited without knowing? Lock them in place so that only you can make changes.

Just The Way You Like It.

Customise your note board by changing the colours, size and position. Have an urgent task? Make it red, move it to the front and lock it in place so you can’t forget!

Free Forever.

We believe great apps should be available to everyone, therefore we plan to keep Notetiles free for as long as we can. We hope you will support us by continuing to use our great little app.
